Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) is a northern Indian union territory famed for its snow-capped mountains, serene valleys, and crystal lakes. It’s culturally rich and a top destination for nature lovers.

  1. Srinagar — Houseboats on Dal Lake
  2. Gulmarg — Gondola Rides and Skiing
  3. Pahalgam — The Lidder Valley Trails
  4. Sonamarg — Thajiwas Glacier Views
  5. Jammu — Vaishno Devi Pilgrimage
