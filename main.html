Jammu & Kashmir


Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) is a region located in the northernmost part of India, historically a state and now a union territory. It's renowned for its stunning natural beauty, including snow-capped mountains, valleys, and lakes, making it a popular tourist destination. Geographically, it's divided into Jammu, Kashmir Valley, and Ladakh. J&K is known for its diverse culture, horticulture, and unique craftwork.
